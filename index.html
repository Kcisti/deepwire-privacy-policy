Privacy Policy - DeepWire NX

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Kcisti's Enterprise operates the DeepWire NX mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Privacy and security are the foundation of DeepWire NX. The application is designed to operate in a decentralized manner and utilizes Symmetric Passphrase-Based Encryption (PSK). The security of your communications relies entirely on the strength and complexity of the PIN you choose to share with your peers. We do not collect, save, or analyze the contents of your communications.

1. Data We Collect and How We Use It

Given the encrypted and anonymous nature of the app, we collect the bare minimum required to operate the Service. We do not require registration via email or phone number.

A. Messages, Media, and Encryption

All text messages, voice notes, images, documents, and GPS locations shared through the app are encrypted locally on your device using the AES-256-GCM standard. The encryption key is dynamically derived from your shared PIN using PBKDF2 with unique cryptographic salts.

  • Data travels in an unreadable format through our routing nodes (MQTT Brokers) for the sole purpose of being delivered to the recipient.
  • No plaintext data is saved on our servers. Chat history is stored exclusively locally on your device using IndexedDB (a secure local database engine).

B. Device Permissions (Sensitive Data)

To provide core features, the app requests access to specific device APIs. Access is granted only with your explicit consent:

  • Camera and Gallery: Used exclusively to take photos or select images from your device to send them (encrypted) within the chat.
  • Microphone: Used exclusively to record voice notes. The audio is processed locally and sent in an encrypted format.
  • Location (GPS): If you choose to share your location in a chat, the app will access your GPS data. The location is transformed into an encrypted link and sent to the recipient. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Storage/Files: Used to allow you to send documents or save received files to your device.
  • Biometric Data (Face ID / Fingerprint): The app uses your device's native biometric APIs to lock access to specific rooms. Your fingerprint or facial data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

C. Device Identifiers and Push Notifications

To ensure you receive messages when the app is in the background, we generate a random unique ID (Push ID associated with your device). This ID is not linked to your real identity but allows our notification service provider to "wake up" your device when a message arrives.

2. Third-Party Services

We share information in a limited manner and only with essential providers for the technical operation of the app:

  • OneSignal: We use OneSignal to manage push notifications. OneSignal may collect standard device information (such as model, OS version, and Push Token). We encourage you to review the OneSignal Privacy Policy.
  • Network Infrastructure (MQTT/Cloudflare): Encrypted data packets pass through intermediary servers necessary for message exchange. These servers cannot decrypt the content of the communications in any way.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

  • On your device: You have total control over your data. The app features commands (e.g., the "Wipe" button or the `/wipe` command) that instantly and irreversibly destroy all cryptographic keys, messages, and settings from your device's memory. Deleting the app also permanently erases all data.
  • On our servers: Because there is no central database, we do not store your messages. Undelivered (offline) messages are held temporarily and encrypted by the network broker only for the time necessary for delivery, after which they are overwritten.

4. User Rights (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

Because DeepWire NX is completely anonymous and we do not store your personal data on our servers, we have no way to access, extract, or delete your information remotely. Therefore, to exercise your right to be forgotten or to erase your data, you must use the deletion features (such as the Wipe function) directly within the app on your device.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is our priority. The app's infrastructure relies on the fact that the cryptographic key is derived from the PIN you enter. Please note: Since the app uses symmetric encryption based on a pre-shared passphrase, sharing a weak PIN or sharing it over insecure channels can compromise the privacy of your room. If you lose your PIN or the device is reset, the encrypted data will be unrecoverable, even by us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
